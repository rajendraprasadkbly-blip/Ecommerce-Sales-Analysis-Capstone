# Business Requirements Document (BRD)
## Project: E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

### 1. Purpose
To provide a consolidated and interactive dashboard that helps management understand sales trends, product performance, customer behavior, and regional sales distribution.

### 2. Business Problem
The business lacks a centralized, data-driven view of:
- Monthly sales trends
- Category-wise performance
- Region-wise growth
- Customer segment contribution

### 3. Objectives
- Improve visibility of sales performance
- Identify top-performing and low-performing categories
- Provide insights for marketing and inventory decisions
- Track customer segments and repeat patterns

### 4. Stakeholders
- Sales Manager
- Marketing Team
- Product Managers
- Directors / Leadership

### 5. Deliverables
- Power BI Dashboard
- Cleaned dataset
- User stories
- Acceptance criteria
- Functional requirements document (FRD)
